About AIEPRO

Philippines' premier English proficiency institute, TESDA accredited with 15+ years of excellence

Founded by Vin Magbuhos, the American Institute for English Proficiency has been transforming lives through English education since its inception. We specialize in English proficiency, communication skills, grammar, writing, American accent training, confidence building, IELTS/TOEFL preparation, critical thinking, personality development, career development, and public speaking.

Our TESDA-accredited programs combine traditional classroom excellence with cutting-edge online learning through our LEO (Learn English Online) platform. With over 77 pages of educational content and thousands of success stories, we continue to be the trusted choice for English education in the Philippines.
